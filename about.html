About

  • Hi I’m Vylan! I’m a Vietnamese-Canadian interactive designer based in Vancouver, Canada. What I like best about UX/UI is the process, the meticulous step-by-step pathway to creating work that makes people’s lives easier—and maybe even more pleasant. When I’m not clicking away on my computer, I can be found sketching with ballpoint pens, reading a thrilling book (mystery and fantasy novels are my favourites), or learning new languages (French and Japanese are my current additions to Vietnamese). I am about to finish my final year at the IDEA School of Design and am looking to work on interactive projects in a studio just like yours.

    If you’re looking for a meticulous UX/UI designer or have a life-changing book recommendation, we should chat. I’m all ears!

    (BTW, you might have been wondering how my name is pronounced. I’ve gotten everything from villain to violin. It’s Vee-Lan.)
