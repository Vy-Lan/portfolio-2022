The Potluck Truck

Social Enterprise Website

The Potluck Truck offers culinary workshops and work experience for refugees in Metro Vancouver. In addition to the Potluck Truck, there is a website that has everything refugees need to know about enrolling in a wide range of workshops. It also shows the monthly menu and changing locations of the food truck where different cultural food traditions and communities come together.
